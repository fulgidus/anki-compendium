## Tips for Marketing Campaign Decks

### Structure

1. **Objectives** ‚Äî What are we trying to achieve?
2. **Audience** ‚Äî Who are we targeting?
3. **Concept** ‚Äî What's the big idea?
4. **Strategy** ‚Äî How will we execute?
5. **Timeline** ‚Äî When does it happen?
6. **Budget** ‚Äî How much will it cost?
7. **Metrics** ‚Äî How will we measure success?

### Content Tips

- **Data-Driven:** Use metrics and benchmarks
- **Visual:** Show mockups, examples, visuals
- **Specific:** Avoid vague statements, be concrete
- **Aligned:** Connect to business goals
- **Realistic:** Set achievable targets

### Design Principles

- **Brand-Aligned:** Use brand colors and fonts
- **Clear Hierarchy:** Headlines, subheads, body
- **Visual Variety:** Mix text, images, charts
- **Consistent:** Same layouts and styles throughout
