## Tips for Pitch Decks

### Structure (10-15 slides, 10-20 minutes)

1. **Title** (1 slide)
2. **Problem** (1-2 slides)
3. **Solution** (1-2 slides)
4. **Market** (1 slide)
5. **Product** (1-2 slides)
6. **Traction** (1-2 slides)
7. **Business Model** (1 slide)
8. **Competition** (1 slide)
9. **Go-to-Market** (1 slide)
10. **Team** (1 slide)
11. **Financials** (1 slide)
12. **The Ask** (1 slide)

### Design Principles

- **Clarity:** One idea per slide
- **Visual:** Use charts, images, icons
- **Concise:** Minimal text, bullet points
- **Professional:** Consistent branding
- **Storytelling:** Build a narrative arc

### Content Tips

- Lead with the problem (pain sells)
- Quantify everything (metrics matter)
- Show, don't tell (demos > descriptions)
- Prove traction (data > promises)
- Be realistic (avoid hype)
- Know your numbers (memorize key metrics)
